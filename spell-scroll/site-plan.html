Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to be a tool for Dungeons and Dragon's Spellcasters. This site will be an easy to set up and see your spell list, available spell slots, and other key information that changes throughout combat and play.

Audience

My audience is Dungeon Masters and Players for Dungeons and Dragons who are playing a character with spells.

Dynamic elements

In the page, you will be able to input your class level, and spellcasting abiltity. It will then populate your available spellslots which you can toggle to show use. You will also be able to select which spells you have prepared from the list provided, which is based on your class. Also an attack roll button to have a pop up spell attack roll

Content

Home page

Welcome to Spell-Scroll, your web tool for spellcasting in D&D 5.5e. On the Spell-Scroll page, you can input your current class, level, and spellcasting modifier and the page will populate your spellslots for you. To choose your spells, select them from the dropdown provided, which is sorted to only see the spells for your class. Use the toggles to show slots used and the long rest button to reset all of those. Clicking on a spell in your list will also bring up it's information at the bottom of the page. Thank you, and may all your hits be crits!
